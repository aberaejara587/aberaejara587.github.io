Biography

My name is Abera Ejara. IT 3RD YEAR at AU. I was born and raised in Ambo, West Shewa Zone, Oromia Region, Ethiopia.

During my childhood, I helped my family by herding cattle and farming. I enjoyed working with my family and spending time playing with my friends. These experiences taught me responsibility, teamwork, and the value of hard work.

I started my education at a young age and continued with strong dedication. After successfully completing Grade 12, I joined Arsi University to pursue higher education. Today, I am working hard to develop my knowledge and skills so that I can build a better future for myself and contribute positively to my community.

I am passionate about learning new technologies and improving my abilities in web development and programming. I enjoy editing multimedia content and learning modern IT tools.

I have experience in HTML, CSS, C++, beginner-level JavaScript, Multimedia Editing, and Computer Maintenance, graphics design, Video editing and photo editing. I designed this website as part of my web development training project.
